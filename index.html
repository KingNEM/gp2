AKAN NAMING



Its an application that calculates the day a user is born depending on their gender and then outputs their Akan name

Akan names are derived from the Ghanian culture to name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person. Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status, or an ancestor's middle name. The Akan do not name a child until a child's been alive for seven days. The feeling is that the baby might be a spirit who has come to look at the world and then go back. Below are the Ghanian Akan day names:

  • Monday:Kwadwo
  • Tuesday:Kwabena

  • Thursday:Yaw
  • Friday:Kofi
  • Saturday:Kwame

Female

  • Monday:Adwoa

  • Wednesday:Akua
